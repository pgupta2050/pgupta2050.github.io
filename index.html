About Me

I am a Ph.D student at Clemson University - ICAR and my research focus is robotics and controls. Specifically, I study combined utilization of model-based and deep learning-based control techniques to improve vehicle behavior under unknowns.

You can view all of my research and development experiences, publications, and awards on my CV page and my key projects on the Projects page.

Key Research Questions and Efforts

"How can we leverage the individual strengths of model-based and learning-based methods to improve off-road driving controls and planning algorithms under unknown dynamics?"
