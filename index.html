About Me

I am a Ph.D student at Clemson University - ICAR and my research focus is robotics and controls. Specifically, I study combined utilization of model based and deep learning based control techniques to improve behavior of vehicle under unknowns.

You can view all of my research and development experiences, publications and awards in my CV here.
